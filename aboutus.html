Our Story

Located in the vibrant heart of Thamel, Kathmandu, TimberArt Nepal has established itself as a leader in sustainable craftsmanship. We are passionate about providing our customers with top-quality, eco-friendly timber products both locally and internationally.

Our goal is to embrace eco-friendly practices while meeting the increasing demand for high-quality handcrafted products. Backed by a dedicated team of professional artisans and advanced sustainable techniques, we ensure every piece meets the highest standards of excellence.

Sustainable Sourcing

All our timber comes from responsibly managed forests with FSC certification, ensuring environmental preservation.

Skilled Artisans

Generations of Nepalese craftsmanship combined with modern techniques create timeless masterpieces.

Global Delivery

Safe and secure worldwide shipping with expert packaging to preserve the beauty of every piece.
